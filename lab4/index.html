Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

This lab had us thinking of lists for how to do an every task and how to make a rock-paper-scissors game on the computer. Then, we created pseudocode for these tasks. The lecture for pseudocoding was very helpful in showing us the process.

Problems

We did not have many problems with this lab. The only minor issues we had were making sure the formatting of our lists was right and coming up with all the steps we would need to take to make pizza and a rock-paper-scissors game.

Reflection

This lab helped us familiarize ourselves with the process of pseudocoding, which will let us better plan out code in the future. We had to think very hard about what steps we would need to put on the lists. We also learned how to center an image on the page with CSS by looking at w3schools.com
